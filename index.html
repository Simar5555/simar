About Us

Simac Industrial Machines based in Ludhiana, Punjab, India, is a Manufacturer, Exporter and Importer of Sewing Machines, Sewing Machine Parts, Motors, Tables and Stands. We also deal in Cutting Machines and Steam Iron. We are in business since 1990 and cater to the sewing industry, providing essential equipment for various applications.

Our Story
In the bustling textile hub of Ludhiana, a small workshop hummed with the rhythmic clatter of sewing machines. Gurneet Singh, a passionate entrepreneur, founded Simac Industrial Machines with a vision: to revolutionize precision stitching. Simac’s journey began modestly, but their commitment to quality and innovation set them apart. As the years passed, Simac expanded its portfolio. Their sturdy sewing machines became the backbone of countless workshops, supporting artisans as they wove dreams into fabric. Simac’s logo – a needle and thread forming an elegant infinity loop – symbolized their unwavering commitment to excellence. Simac’s machines found homes in couture boutiques, garment factories and studios. Their success story echoed across India and world. Today, Simac Industrial Machines stands tall, a beacon of craftsmanship. Their legacy is stitched into every fabric, every seam – a testament to vision and the tireless hands that wield Simac’s tools.
